Here's a timeline of Taylor Swift's life and major accomplishments:

  • 1989 - Born in West Reading, Pennsylvania
  • 2004 - Convinces her parents to move to Memphis, Tennessee so she can pursue a career in country music
  • 2006 - Releases her debut album - self titled asTaylor Swift
    • Taylor Swift becomes the longest-charting album of the 2000s on the Billboard 200
    • Taylor Swift becomes the youngest person to write and perform a #1 song on the BillboardHot Country Songs chart
  • 2008 - Releases her second album - Fearless
    • Fearless wins Grammy for Album of the Year and is certified Diamond by the Recording Industry Association of America
  • 2010 - Releases her third album - Speak Now
    • Speak Now songs Mine and Back to December become top-10 singles
  • 2012- Releases her fourth album - Red
  • 2014- Releases her fifth album -1989, making a full transistion to pop music
